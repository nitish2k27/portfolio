About Me

I'm Nitish Patil, currently pursuing my BTech in Computer Science Engineering at RV University, Bengaluru, with an AI & ML major. I enjoy building web applications and exploring machine learning, with a focus on understanding how systems work end to end.

What motivates me most is the process itself — brainstorming an idea, visualizing the end product, and then reaching it through coding, debugging, and refinement. Turning a concept into something functional and reliable is what keeps me engaged.

I started learning with Java in my first year, but web development quickly caught my attention. Along the way, I began exploring AI and machine learning, looking for ways to combine both with practical applications. Currently, I'm focused on strengthening my full-stack development skills while diving deeper into machine learning use cases.
